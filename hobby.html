My interests

Work related interests

Building a project from scratch

Why building a project from scratch? Because I like to see the work that I’ve done and the progress of something. I can really get complacence from seeing or using something that I’ve made myself. Besides that it encourages me to continue my work to reach the result.

Inspire/help other developers

Inspiring or helping people because I like to see people grow. With helping people you can learn them something but in the other way around I can also learn from other people. So I guess helping someone can work in two ways.

Interests at home

Climbing

Climbing, more specific ‘bouldering’ is my favourite sport. It’s a sport at which I’m good and I can see progress by doing it for a couple times a week. In my spare time I’m living a very active life. It keeps me fit and gets me a clear head afterwards. I really don’t like to surf on the couch all day watching television.

`Take your work home` mentality

Why a ‘take your work home’ mentality? I’m not the type of person who just goes to work and leaves it there. Which doesn’t mean that I’m working on a project until I go to sleep. I want to say, I’m always available when colleagues have a problem at work. They can always call me. I will do the best I can to help them. Also doing overtime is not a problem for me.
